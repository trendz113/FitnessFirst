4–5 days a week. Always 3–4 min slow warm-up before, 3–4 min stretch after — that alone prevents most cramps.

Walking progression (build up, don't jog yet)

Dumbbell strength — legs & back

2–3 days a week, not back-to-back days. Pick a weight where the last 2 reps of each set feel hard but your form stays clean. 2 sets to start, add a 3rd after 2 weeks.

Goblet squat

Dumbbell at chest, sit hips back, chest up10 reps × 2–3

Dumbbell RDL

Flat back, hinge hips back, dumbbells slide down shins10 reps × 2–3

Reverse lunge

Step back, back knee taps light, drive through front heel8 each side × 2

Bent-over row

Hinge forward, pull dumbbell to ribs, squeeze back10 each side × 2–3

Farmer carry

Dumbbells at sides, walk tall 20–30 steps, tight core3 rounds

Weighted calf raise

Dumbbells at sides, rise onto toes, control the lower15 reps × 2
If pain radiates down a leg, causes numbness or tingling, or sharpens during any of these — stop and see a doctor or physio. These are for general stiffness, not a diagnosis.
